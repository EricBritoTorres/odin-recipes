Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Ingredients to make grilled cheese sandwiches
  3. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
  4. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
  5. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  6. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  7. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese. Serve and enjoy!
