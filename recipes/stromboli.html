Stromboli

Description

Stromboli is a delicious Italian-American dish made with pizza dough, filled with various ingredients, and rolled up into a log shape. It's perfect for parties or a cozy night in!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into a rectangle.
  3. Spread marinara sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Layer mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, and bell peppers on top of the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the filling.
  6. Roll the dough tightly from one end to the other, sealing the edges.
  7. Place the rolled stromboli seam-side down on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Brush the top with beaten egg for a golden finish.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve with marinara sauce for dipping!
